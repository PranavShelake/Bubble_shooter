How to play

Game Instructions:

  • Look at the number displayed in the hit block.
  • Your goal is to find and click the bubble with the same number as quickly as possible.
  • Each correct click earns you +10 points.
  • Each incorrect click results in a -10 points penalty.
  • You have a total of 30 seconds to score as many points as possible.

Good luck!
